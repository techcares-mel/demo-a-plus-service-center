About Us

Sunshine's Honest Mechanic

A Plus Service Centre has been serving the Sunshine community with integrity and expertise. Led by mechanic Sam, our team is committed to providing transparent, high-quality automotive care at fair prices.

What sets us apart is our commitment to honest communication. We take the time to explain exactly what's wrong with your vehicle and what it will cost — no pressure, no unnecessary upselling. Whether it's a routine oil change or a full engine replacement, every job gets the same level of care and attention to detail.

We're proud to be a local, family-focused service centre that the Sunshine community trusts. Our customers keep coming back — and they refer their friends — because they know they'll be treated fairly every time.
